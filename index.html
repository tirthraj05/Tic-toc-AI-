How to Play Tic Tac Toe Online

Tic Tac Toe is a simple strategy game played on a 3 x 3 grid. You play as X and the computer plays as O. Tap any empty box to place your move. The first player to get three marks in a row horizontally, vertically or diagonally wins the game.

This version of Tic Tac Toe runs directly in your browser, works on mobile and desktop, and lets you reset the board instantly.
